The Top Five Best "The Office" Characters

The List:

#1. Michael Scott
The one who starts the show, Michael Gary Scott plays an astronomical part in what makes "The Office" so great. Steve Carrel does a wonderful job portraying him, as he really made Michael his own. His antics were the best part of the show and he is one of, if not the most, memorable character.
#2. Jim Halpert
Although I am quite torn between placing Jim or Dwight in this spot, I chose Jim because he made so many key events happen in the show. He gave us a great love story and caused Dwight to be picked as regional manager in season nine. He also had well written character growth, so for all those reasons he is number two.
#3. Dwight Schrute
As the most intense character in "The Office", Dwight sets an example of what it is like to be goal oriented. His ability to be hilarious with even realizing is one of the many reasons that Dwight is in the top three. His enemy to friend storyline with Jim is one of my favorites and his willingness to protect the ones he loves is what makes him a favorite among fans.
